Madden Mobile Boost Calculator

Instructions

Enter the overall of each of your team's players into their respective sections below.

After each position has been entered, select the boost level you would like to see. If you want to do a single boost level for every position group, use the "total boost" slider. Otherwise move each individual slider to the desired level.

Once your players have been entered and you've chosen your boost levels, you can then press the "Calculate" button to view your estimated boosted overall, as well as the amount of coins and weights it will cost.

Disclaimer

This app is still in development, and as such there are a few bugs I should mention.

First off, weight room boosts only apply to a player's base overall. That is to say that if a player has been leveled up at all, the boosted overall provided by this app will be overestimated. If possible, when entering your players' overall, use their base overall (level 0) to the exact decimal point value. The weight and coin cost are uneffected by this and should be perfectly accurate.

I'm currently working on developing a complete database of all of the players in the game so that rather than entering an overall, you should be able to just select the exact card which in turn will allow a perfectly accurate boost calculator.
